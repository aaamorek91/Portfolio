A Peek into My Tip Calculation Ptogram


Ever found yourself perplexed when totting up tips for your bills? I recently embarked on simplifying this process by creating a straightforward JavaScript program. Let me take you through what I did:


The Code Breakdown:



  • 1. const preTip = 670;
    I set a variable (preTip) to represent the amount before any tip is added. You can replace this with the actual total from your bill.
  • 2. const tipPercent = 10;
    Here, I defined the tip percentage as 10%. Adjust this based on your preferred tipping rate.
  • 3. const tipAmount = (preTip * tipPercent) / 100;
    Using a simple formula, I calculated the tip amount by multiplying the pre-tip total with the tip percentage and dividing by 100.
  • 4. const totalBill = preTip + tipAmount;
    I then calculated the total bill amount by adding the pre-tip total and the calculated tip amount.
  • 5. console.log(...);
    Finally, I used console.log to display the results. The total bill and tip amount are presented with a precision of two decimal places.
